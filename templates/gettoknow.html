Get to Know Taehyung & Jungkook

Discover the lives, talents, and stories of BTS's iconic members, Taehyung (V) and Jungkook (Golden Maknae).

About Taehyung

Name: Kim Taehyung (V)

Birth Date: December 30, 1995

Birth Place: Daegu, South Korea

About: Taehyung is one of BTS's most beloved members, known for his deep, soulful voice, unique personality, and artistic talents in singing, dancing, and photography.

Fun Facts

  • Favorite Color: Purple
  • Favorite Food: Samgyupsal (Grilled Pork Belly)
  • Special Skill: Photography and Painting

About Jungkook

Name: Jeon Jungkook (Golden Maknae)

Birth Date: September 1, 1997

Birth Place: Busan, South Korea

About: Jungkook is the youngest member of BTS and is known for his versatile talents in singing, dancing, songwriting, and producing. His songs like "Euphoria" and "My Time" have topped global charts.

Fun Facts

  • Favorite Color: Red
  • Favorite Food: Chicken
  • Special Skill: Art and Photography
