Why Voting is Important

Voting helps Taehyung and Jung Kook secure prestigious awards, such as those from MAMA, Billboard, and other global music platforms. It showcases fan dedication and gives them global recognition.

Popular Platforms for Voting

  • Mnet Plus: MAMA Awards voting. Create an account and vote daily during the voting period.
  • Fan N Star: Vote regularly for features like birthday ads or awards like The Fact Music Awards.
  • Billboard Fan Voting: Participate in Billboard voting for categories like Top Social Artist.
  • Twitter Voting: Many award shows count hashtag votes. Use hashtags like #V, #Jungkook, or #Taekook along with award-specific tags during voting periods.

How to Maximize Voting Impact

  • Create multiple accounts on voting platforms using different email addresses.
  • Join fanbases or groups that organize mass voting events.
  • Check daily voting limits and ensure consistency.
  • Spread awareness on social media to encourage others to vote.

Preparing for Voting Seasons

Most major voting events occur around year-end awards or comeback promotions. Keep these tips in mind:

  • Download and register on voting apps like Mnet Plus, Idol Champ, and Whosfan in advance.
  • Stockpile voting credits or points by completing app missions early.
  • Follow reliable fanbases for updates and deadlines.
