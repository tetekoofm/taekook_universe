Buying Official Albums & Merchandise

Purchasing from legitimate sources ensures your support counts for charting and directly benefits Taehyung and Jung Kook.

Why Buying Matters

Purchasing official albums and merchandise contributes to chart rankings, supports their careers financially, and encourages more creative projects in the future. Physical album sales, in particular, are a major factor in determining awards and achievements.

Where to Buy Official Albums

  • Weverse Shop: The official global store for BTS merchandise. It ensures your purchase contributes to charts like Gaon and Hanteo.
  • Ktown4u: A trusted source for international fans. Albums purchased here often count for both Hanteo and Gaon charts.
  • Local Stores: Look for official partnerships with stores like Target, Walmart, and Amazon in your region.
  • iTunes: For digital albums, ensure you download and don’t just stream. Digital purchases contribute to charts like Billboard and iTunes rankings.

Tips for Buying Merchandise

Fan-made merchandise is amazing, but official merchandise directly supports Taehyung and Jung Kook. Here are some trusted platforms:

  • Weverse Shop: Offers high-quality official merchandise like photo books, apparel, and collectibles.
  • Hybe Insight Store: Limited-edition items directly curated by their agency.
  • BigHit Entertainment Store: Special releases for concerts, anniversaries, and more.

Buying Tickets for Events

Attending their concerts, fan meets, and live events is a dream for many fans. Here’s how to get legitimate tickets:

  • Ticketmaster: For international events, tickets are typically released here. Be sure to sign up early for presales.
  • Interpark: Used for events hosted in South Korea. Requires an account and familiarity with Korean language or translators.
  • Local Organizers: For specific regions, verify the authenticity of the organizers before purchasing tickets.

Key Reminders for Buying

  • Always double-check the source to ensure it’s legitimate and supports official charting.
  • Join fanbases or local fan groups for information on group orders and bulk purchases.
  • Beware of scalpers and overpriced items—support official channels only.
  • Keep an eye out for presale announcements and join waitlists early.
