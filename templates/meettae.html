About Taehyung

Kim Taehyung, better known as V, is a South Korean singer, songwriter, and actor. He is one of the lead vocalists of the globally renowned K-pop group BTS. Known for his deep voice and unique artistic expression, Taehyung has also ventured into acting, having appeared in the popular Korean drama 'Hwarang.' His passion for art extends beyond music, with a love for painting and photography. 🐯

He is known for his fun-loving, yet deep personality. His love for the arts, including his incredible vocal range, his sense of humor, and his emotional performances, have made him a global icon. V is also recognized for his warm and friendly interaction with fans, which has earned him a massive, dedicated following worldwide. 🐯
