Who is Kim Taehyung?

⬇️

🐻 Meet Kim Taehyung 🐯

Kim Taehyung 김태형, known as his stage name V, is a South Korean singer, songwriter, record producer and actor. He is a member of BTS, as well as a successful solo artist. He is a baritone, known for his wide vocal range and unique musicality. He is heavily influenced by his love for jazz and classical music. As a dancer and performer he is recognized worldwide for his engaging stage presence and versatility.

“I love you more than yesterday, but less than tomorrow.” — V
⬇️

🌱 From Daegu to Debut

Born on December 30, 1995, in Daegu, South Korea, Kim Taehyung was raised in a loving family with strong values. As the eldest of three children, he grew up under the warmth of his grandparents, who played a pivotal role in shaping his early life. He graduated from Korean Arts High School in 2014. He also received his degree in Broadcasting and Entertainment from the Global Cyber University in 2020. Tae has shown his love for music from a young age. He picked up the saxophone in middle school and fell in love with the way it made him feel.

“I want to become a singer that my fans can be proud of.” — V

Taehyung auditioned for Big Hit in 2011, purely by chance. He was only accompanying a friend. A staff member encouraged him to try out, and he ended up becoming a trainee. While other members were introduced early, V remained a mystery to fans until the very day BTS debuted. His first appearance sparked curiosity and admiration. That deep voice! That gaze! That energy! He may have been the last surprise of the debut line-up, but he quickly proved himself as an irreplaceable piece of the BTS puzzle.

⬇️

🎤 The BTS Years

V made his debut with BTS on June 13, 2013. He was credited for music composition on many BTS songs, first being ‘Hold Me Tight’ from the album ‘The Most Beautiful Moment in Life, Part 1.” The first solo single he composed for BTS was ‘Stigma’ from the album “Wings”. His second solo song with BTS, ‘Singularity,’ was released in May 2018, on their third LP, “Love Yourself: Tear.” ‘Singularity earned critical acclaim, including being ranked #20 on the “65 Best Songs of 2018’ by the New York Times.

As a solo artist, he released his first self-composed song, ‘Scenery’ on January 30, 2019 via SoundCloud. It broke the daily streaming record nine times during its first two weeks of release. Seven months later, he released ‘Winter Bear’ on August 9, 2019, along with a self-directed music video published on BTS’s YouTube page.

He followed those up with ‘Sweet Night’ in March 2020, a song he wrote and performed for the soundtrack of the JTBC drama ‘Itaewon Class.’ ‘Sweet Night’ debuted at number two on Billboard’s U.S. Digital Song sales chart, the highest debut for a K-soloist in the chart’s history. Then on December 25, 2020, he released another solo track, ‘Snow Flower’ featuring Peakboy. He released his third OST on December 21, 2021 with ‘Christmas Tree.’ It was written for the South Korean television series ‘Our Beloved Summer.’

“Sometimes, it is okay to take a break, take a step back and reflect on yourself.” — V

💜 V's Legacy: “Borahae”

V is also credited with coining the phrase, “Borahae (보라해) - I purple you” in November 2016 - a heartfelt expression of trust and love. Since then, purple has become a symbol of BTS and their fans’ unbreakable bond, even inspiring campaign's like BTS’s anti-bullying collaboration with UNICEF.

“Purple is the last of the rainbow colors, so it means i will love and trust you for a long time.” — V
⬇️

🎬 Acting Career

Taehyung possesses a rare and unforgettable aura, making him effortlessly unique and setting him apart from everyone else. Not to forget, he is ambidextrous, a small but remarkable detail that reflects his natural versatility.

Taehyung’s love for acting has always been a quiet part of his artistry. Long before his official debut, he often created skits and playful roleplays with fellow BTS members, showing his natural expressiveness and imagination.

In 2016, he made his acting debut in the historical drama ‘Hwarang: The Poet Warrior Youth’, where he portrayed the warm-hearted warrior Hansung. His sincerity and innocence on screen touched many viewers. Taehyung also contributed to the drama’s soundtrack, collaborating with his fellow BTS member, Jin on the heartfelt song ‘It’s Definitely You’ in 2017, blending his love for music and acting into one beautiful moment.

“Don’t be trapped in someone else’s dream.” — V
⬇️

🐾 Introducing: Kim Yeontan

Taehyung is also well-known for his love of his late dog, Kim Yeontan, a pomeranian first introduced to fans in December 2017. Yeontan or "Tannie" was a huge part of Taehyung's life.

Tannie was cited as his muse for his debut album, ‘Layover.” His image graced the cover of the album, and he is featured in the music videos earning him The K-Pup Idol title.

V also used Yeontan’s image for the cover art for his collaboration with Bing Crosby, “White Christmas.” The music video also featured a cartoon Yeontan, ending in an image of Tannie in the stars. Taehyung later announced the passing of Yeontan in late 2024.

“Let’s make precious memories again as we always have. I really miss you, and I really love you.” — V

🎨 The Culture of Tae

Beyond the spotlight, Taehyung finds solace and inspiration in various forms of art. Aa an avid photographer, he often shares his work under the pseudonym “Vante”, a nod to photographer Ante Badzim. His photos are quiet, thoughtful reflections of how he sees the world — tender, detailed, and deeply emotional.

He also enjoys video games, where he relaxes, explores, and shares small moments with fans in his own quiet way. For Taehyung, art isn’t just a hobby, but a reflection of his inner self. He doesn’t just create; he curates meaningful experiences.

His SoundCloud releases, like "Scenery" and "Winter Bear," blend his visuals and vocals. With heartfelt lyrics, ambient sounds, and self-shot visuals, these tracks revealed an introspective and poetic side of Taehyung. As a member of BTS, he brings depth to their music with his rich baritone and stands out as a captivating stage performer, known for his charisma and emotional expression.

“I want to be someone who can express things that can’t be said with words.” — V
⬇️

🎶 The Voice of V: Solo Era

V released his first solo album, “Layover” on September 8, 2023.

Layover consists of six tracks in total - five tracks and a bonus track. From soulful ballads to sleek R&B rhythms, Layover captures V’s introspective journey. To fully appreciate the album's flow, Taehyung recommends listening to it in sequence from start to finish.

Tracklist:

  • Love Me Again
  • Rainy Days
  • Slow Dancing
  • Blue
  • For Us
  • Slow Dancing Piano

‘Love Me Again’ and ‘Rainy Days’ were pre-released on August 11, 2023. ’Slow Dancing’ is the Title Track of the album.

The entire album felt like a letter to the fans. During promotions, he appeared on variety shows charming audiences with his natural charisma. From Tiny Desk Korea to ELLE and Vogue interviews, he proved that V, the artist was just as magnetic as V the idol. With each movement, each look, he reminded the world: he isn’t just singing the music. He *is* the music.

Some of the achievements the album received are:

  • The highest first day sales first-day sales of any solo album in Hanteo history, at $2,101,974 copies.
  • #1 on iTunes Top Albums chart in 65 regions. ‘Slow Dancing’ reached #1 on iTunes Top Songs in 75 regions.
  • #2 on Billboard’s Top 25 K-Pop Albums of 2023.
“I just want to show you my true self. This is who I am.” — V

💌 TaeTae’s Whispers

Taehyung speaks with his fans, listens to them, and comforts them like a friend. He appears spontaneously when he misses them either through his Instagram posts or a very short Weverse Lives or through his replies to fan posts. He creates a space that feels personal, safe, and full of love. He has the record of doing the 8 second short live where he just came to say "Hi" to his fans.

During his live, he asks fans how their day went, plays music, reads comments out loud, and even shows Yeontan curled up beside him. Taehyung being just Taehyung. In his replies to ARMY, he’s witty, playful, and thoughtful. When fans express sadness or insecurity, he gently reminds them of their worth. When they joke, he laughs along. Through the screen, he turns a million voices into a quiet, honest conversation.

“You missed me? That’s why i came. - Kim Taehyung”
