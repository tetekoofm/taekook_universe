What Does Buying Music Mean?

Buying music refers to legally purchasing physical/digital copies of an album or single from authorized platforms or stores respectively. This increases the sales, thus helps with the artist's chart rankings.

How to Buy Music?

Follow these steps to ensure your purchase is counted:

  • Choose the Right Platform: Purchase from official platforms like Weverse Shop, BTS Store, iTunes, Qobuz, Amazon, and Ktown4u.
  • Buy Individual Tracks & Albums: On digital platforms, buy individual songs first to maximize impact and then proceed to buy the album.
  • Avoid Bulk Purchases in One Go: Spread out your purchases using different accounts and payment methods instead of bulk-buying at once.
  • Use Different Platforms: Purchase from multiple platforms instead of focusing on just one.
  • Ensure Proper Payment: Use legitimate payment methods to avoid refunds or cancellations that could affect charting.
  • Download Immediately: After digital purchases, download the songs/albums immediately, to ensure the sale is counted. Also, delete them from your Library so your streams are counted.
  • Share & Recommend: Share the official buying links with friends and family to encourage them to purchase.
  • Join Buying Parties: If you are unsure, join the Buying Parties organized by our fanbases during the new release.
