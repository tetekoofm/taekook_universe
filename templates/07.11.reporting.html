What Can Be Reported?

  • Inappropriate Content: Videos, images, posts, or comments that degrade their dignity.
  • Fake Accounts: Profiles especially newly created, pretending to be Taehyung, Jung Kook, or their friends.
  • Unauthorized Merchandise: Items being sold illegally without their permission.
  • Defamatory Posts: False accusations, hate speech, or cyberbullying.
  • Piracy: Unlawful distribution of their music, videos, or performances.

How to Report?

  1. On Social Media:
    • Twitter: Use the "Report Tweet" option for offending posts or accounts.
    • Instagram: Click the three dots on a post or profile, then select "Report."
    • Facebook: Use the "Give Feedback or Report" feature on posts or profiles.
  2. To Official Platforms:
    • Weverse: Report inappropriate posts via the "Report" option in the app.
    • BigHit Music: Email protect@bighitcorp.com with evidence and details.
  3. For Piracy: Submit piracy reports to platforms like YouTube or use the DMCA takedown feature.

Important Tips

  • Be Polite: When reporting, always remain respectful and stick to the facts.
  • Provide Evidence: Attach screenshots, links, or any relevant proof when submitting reports.
  • Be Timely: Reporting early prevents further spread of the issue.
  • Raise Awareness: Inform other fans to report harmful content as a collective effort.

Did You Know?

  • BigHit Music has a dedicated team for monitoring and addressing harmful activities involving BTS members.
  • Collective reporting by fans often leads to quicker resolutions on platforms like YouTube and Twitter.
