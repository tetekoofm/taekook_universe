Why Reporting Matters

What Can Be Reported?

  • Inappropriate Content: Videos, images, posts, or comments that degrade their dignity.
  • Fake Accounts: Profiles especially newly created, pretending to be Taehyung, Jung Kook, or their friends.
  • Unauthorized Merchandise: Items being sold illegally without their permission.
  • Defamatory Posts: False accusations, hate speech, spreading rumors, or cyberbullying.
  • Piracy: Illegal distribution of their music

How to Report?

  • On Weverse: Report inappropriate posts and comments via the "Report" option.
  • BigHit Music: Email protect@bighitcorp.com with evidence and details.
  • On X: Click Here to directly report to X.
  • On Youtube: Click Here to directly report to Youtube.
  • On Other Social Media: Click the three dots on the content/profile and select "Report".

Important Tips

  • Be Responsible: Verify facts and intentions of the author of such posts before reporting. Avoid engaging with clout chasers or getting involved in fan wars. Just mute, report, and block.
  • Be Timely: Reporting early helps prevent the further spread of harmful content. Share it with our fanbase Report for JK & Tae immediately, including a screenshot or direct link to the post.
  • Provide Evidence: Always include screenshots, links, or any relevant proof when submitting reports directly.
  • Raise Awareness: Share with your fellow fans to report harmful content collectively for a stronger impact.

Did You Know?

  • BigHit Music has a dedicated team for monitoring and addressing harmful activities involving BTS members.
  • Collective reporting by fans often leads to quicker resolutions.

If you find anything inappropriate or need help, please reach out to us at Report for JK and Tae.
